Immersion of piles using jack-in technology.

The uniqueness of technology

Our company offers services for the safe immersion of piles, various steel products for the protection of excavations at sites of intra-quarter construction and industrial facilities, including emergency and technogenic contamination.

Experience

Fifteen years of experience with our patented equipment allows us to solve complex tasks of erecting foundations and foundations under various geological conditions.

Work safety

The technique allows you to statically immerse the piles below the day surface without opening the pit, which reduces the risks and time of the open pit. The force of indentation is monitored at each point in time. Despite the capacity of up to 200 tons of force, the equipment has small dimensions and a system of damper hydraulics recoil, which allows working without jolts and vibrations in the immediate vicinity of the structures. Also with the help of our technology it is possible to conduct static tests of a single pile without a beam system, while anchor piles are not needed. There is the possibility of leading heavy piles with puncture technology (without drilling).

Easy transportation

Simplicity of relocation, the possibility of rapid mobilization and entry into work, directly, "from the wheels" allows you to respond quickly to emerged emergency situations in a confined space.

Our Features

The large area, the automatic distribution of the compression force and the constant alignment of the immersed elements, ensures the reliability of the capture and the integrity of the submersible piles. Direct digital force indication 1: 1 without any recalculations and tables at each time point. The possibility of pushing to a greater depth from the surface of the day is the main feature of our work on the safe erection of foundations. Static tests of single piles. There is no need for a beam system.
