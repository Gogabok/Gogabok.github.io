First Steps on Forex

Often, novice traders can not understand how to trade forex? “Correct” trading includes a wide range of different aspects. The most important ones, for example, include:

1. Psychological training of the trader

The psychological factor in trading is of particular importance. Patience, endurance, the ability to control their own emotions and not succumb to the “herd” reflex characteristic of the market “plankton” - these are factors that help the trader to keep any situation is under control.

2. Learning Ability

A trader may be eager to understand the theory of market trading, but if he has problems understanding simple school disciplines, it will be difficult for him. He can easily start trading forex, but the result of his trading can be dubious.

3. The propensity for analysis

Regardless of the results of trading, you need to constantly analyze every working day, every completed transaction. This is important to identify patterns that help / hinder the trading process.

4. Making informed decisions

No one will decide for a trader which lot he should enter the market when it is possible to open / close a position. None will tell him to use one or another trading technique, where and when to apply this or that trading approach. No doubt in the process of learning at some courses such as "forex for beginners", as recommendations, he will be given tips trainer / teacher.

How to trade forex?

The speculation mechanism on the exchange is simple - “buy cheaper”, and “sell more expensive” or vice versa. Remember Forex trading - is making a profit due to the difference between the prices of "purchase / sale" or "Sales / purchases." This is real currency speculation - a commodity that is on a currency exchange some are ready to sell / buy, while others are ready to buy / sell.

Forex Charts. How to read and analyze them?

As you know, in the Forex market, all trading is a method of forecasting prices, based on which lies the price history of the forex market. All trading is based on observations from Forex charts. They play a key role. Forex charts need to be able to read, because it depends on them all your trade as a whole. Forex trading chart consists of two axes: vertical axis and horizontal.

Technical and fundamental analysis of the market and its fundamentals

Technical analysis in the market is forecasting prices based on the history of trading. In the technical The analysis uses data from previous trading days, transaction volumes, demand for currencies and much another. A fundamental analysis of the Forex market is a forecast of prices in foreign exchange markets. The essence of this analysis - to study, for what reason, the price has changed. There are a lot of traders in the foreign exchange market, who prefer this type of analysis.
